FORENSIC SERVICES

Engineered for attorneys, litigation teams, and expert witnesses. We deliver authoritative visual assets designed to withstand courtroom scrutiny, clarify spatial relationships, and make arguments unforgettable. From local scene surveys to nationwide field deployments, we document physical truth under any operational constraints.

SCENE RECONSTRUCTION

Accurate, frame-by-frame spatial reconstructions of vehicular collisions, industrial incidents, and maritime accidents. We deploy on-site to verify physical parameters, integrating telemetry, laser scans, and eyewitness testimony into clear, scale-accurate event timelines.

MITIGATION VIDEOS

Sobering, documentary-grade films developed for sentencing hearings and settlement conferences. We humanize defendants, capture character testimonies, and articulate systemic contexts that paper briefs cannot convey.

DEMONSTRATIVE EVIDENCE

High-impact visuals designed to simplify complex patent claims, mechanical failure modes, or anatomical operations. We convert dry technical data into highly persuasive, jury-admissible exhibits.

FORENSIC STUDY & VERIFICATION

Frame-by-frame clarification, metadata integrity validation, speed-from-video calculation, and camera angle synchronization. We extract and certify objective facts from source surveillance and cell phone footage.
